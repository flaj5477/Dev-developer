<p class="intro">With jQuery, it is easy to add new elements/content.</p>
<h2>Add New HTML Content</h2>
<p>We will look at four jQuery methods that are used to add new content:</p>
<li><code class="w3-codespan">append()</code> - Inserts content at the end of the selected elements</li>
<li><code class="w3-codespan">prepend()</code> - Inserts content at the beginning of the selected elements</li>
<li><code class="w3-codespan">after()</code> - Inserts content after the selected elements</li>
<li><code class="w3-codespan">before()</code> - Inserts content before the selected elements</li>
<h2>jQuery append() Method</h2>
<p>The jQuery <code class="w3-codespan">append()</code> method inserts content AT THE END of the selected HTML elements.</p>

<h2>jQuery prepend() Method</h2>
<p>The jQuery <code class="w3-codespan">prepend()</code> method inserts content AT THE BEGINNING of the selected HTML elements.</p>
<h3>Example</h3>
